# Document Phone Call

Document a phone conversation for the record.

## Instructions

1. **Gather call details** (if not provided):
   - Date and time
   - Who called whom
   - Person's name and role
   - Phone number (if new contact)
   - Duration (approximate)

2. **Document the conversation:**
   - Main topics discussed
   - Key points made by each party
   - Any commitments or promises made
   - Agreed next steps
   - Follow-up actions needed

3. **Create call record** in correspondence/:
   ```markdown
   # Phone Call Record

   **Date:** [Date and time]
   **Duration:** [Approximate length]
   **Participants:** [Names and roles]
   **Call initiated by:** [Who called whom]

   ## Topics Discussed
   [Bullet points of main topics]

   ## Key Points
   - [Name] said: [Important statement]
   - [Name] agreed to: [Commitment]

   ## Commitments Made
   - [ ] [Action item - who - deadline]

   ## Follow-up Required
   - [Next steps with dates]

   ## Notes
   [Any additional context or observations]
   ```

4. **Save and cross-reference:**
   - Save to correspondence/ with date naming
   - Add to correspondence-log.md
   - Add to timeline if significant
   - Update deadlines if commitments made
   - Update contacts if new person

## Important Reminders

- Document calls as soon as possible while details are fresh
- Note exact wording for important statements
- If commitments were made, send a confirmation email
- "I'd like to confirm our conversation in writing" is your friend
