## Important Deadlines to Track

- IEP meeting dates
- Evaluation consent deadlines (typically 15 days)
- Evaluation completion (typically 60 days from consent)
- Annual IEP review dates
- Re-evaluation due dates (every 3 years)
- Response deadlines from district
- Document request deadlines

## Legal Timeline Reminders

- District must respond to evaluation request within reasonable time
- After consent, evaluation must complete within state timeline
- IEP must be developed within 30 days of eligibility determination
- Changes to IEP take effect immediately unless otherwise specified
