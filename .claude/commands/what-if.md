# What-If Advice

Get advice for specific advocacy situations.

## Instructions

1. **Ask what situation the user is facing** (if not provided)

2. **Check advice/ folder** for relevant scenarios:
   - advice/what-if/ - Situation-based quick cards
   - advice/how-to/ - Detailed guides

3. **If matching scenario exists:**
   - Present the relevant advice
   - Offer to customize for their specific situation

4. **If no matching scenario:**
   - Provide guidance based on:
     - IDEA requirements
     - State law (check legal-resources/)
     - General advocacy best practices
   - Offer to create a new what-if card for future reference

5. **For each situation, address:**
   - What you can do
   - What the school is required to do
   - Suggested response/action
   - Language to use
   - Potential escalation paths
   - Documentation needed

## Common Scenarios

- School says "we don't do that"
- School denies evaluation request
- Services not being implemented
- Child regressing despite services
- School wants to reduce services
- Disagreement over goals
- School suggests different placement
- IEP not being followed
- Retaliation concerns
- Communication breakdown
