# Progress Tracking

Monitor your child's progress and document observations.

## Files

- `goals-tracker.md` - Track IEP goal progress
- `services-log.md` - Log services received
- `observations.md` - Your observations at home
- `wins.md` - Celebrate successes

## Tips

- Update goals tracker after each progress report
- Note any services missed or changed
- Document specific examples of challenges or successes
- Review regularly to identify patterns
