## State-Specific Timelines

| Requirement | Federal IDEA | Wisconsin |
|-------------|--------------|-----------|
| Respond to evaluation request | N/A | **15 school days** |
| Complete evaluation | 60 days | 60 days |
| IEP meeting after evaluation | 30 days | 30 days |
| Annual IEP review | 365 days | 365 days |
| Provide PWN | Before action | Before action |

**Note:** Wisconsin's 15 school day response requirement is stricter than federal law.

## State Laws and Regulations

**State Special Education Law:** Wisconsin Statutes Chapter 115, Subchapter V

**State Regulations:** PI 11 (Wisconsin Administrative Code)

**Key Wisconsin-Specific Provisions:**
- 15 school day response to evaluation requests
- State complaint resolution within 60 days
- Strong IEP meeting facilitation program through WSEMS

### Finding a Special Education Attorney

Questions to ask:
- Experience with Wisconsin special education cases?
- Familiarity with PI 11 regulations?
- Have you worked with this school district before?
- Fee structure (hourly, contingency, etc.)?
